Does DineOpen support GST billing in India?

Yes, DineOpen includes GST-ready billing software. It automatically calculates GST, generates compliant invoices, and maintains records required for tax filing. The system supports all GST rates and formats invoices according to Indian tax regulations.

How does GST billing work in DineOpen?

DineOpen GST billing software automatically calculates GST at applicable rates (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) based on item categories. When generating a bill, the system calculates subtotal, applies applicable GST, calculates final total, and generates a tax-compliant invoice with HSN codes.

The system maintains complete tax records including GST collected, input tax credit, and tax liability. All invoices are formatted according to Indian GST regulations and include required details such as GSTIN, HSN codes, tax rates, and tax amounts.

GST Billing Features

Automatic GST Calculation

System automatically calculates GST at applicable rates based on item categories. Supports all GST slabs: 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%.

Compliant Invoice Generation

Generate tax-compliant invoices with HSN codes, GSTIN, tax rates, and tax amounts. Invoices formatted according to Indian GST regulations.

Tax Record Maintenance

Maintain complete tax records including GST collected, input tax credit, and tax liability. Records required for GST filing.

Data Export for Filing

Export tax data in formats compatible with GST filing systems. Generate reports for monthly and annual GST returns.

What GST rates does DineOpen support?

DineOpen GST billing software supports all GST rates applicable in India: 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. The system also supports 0% GST for exempt items and can handle different GST rates for different items in the same bill.

Restaurants can configure GST rates for different item categories. The system automatically applies the correct GST rate when generating bills, ensuring accuracy and compliance with Indian tax regulations.
