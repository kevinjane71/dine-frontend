How does online order management work for restaurants?

Online order management processes orders from WhatsApp, QR menus, and web platforms. Orders appear in real-time on the POS system, can be accepted or rejected, and are automatically sent to the kitchen via KOT. The system tracks order status from placement to delivery.

How does DineOpen online order management work?

DineOpen online order management receives orders from multiple channels including WhatsApp, QR menus, web platforms, and delivery apps. When a customer places an order, it appears in real-time on the POS system. Restaurant staff can accept or reject the order, and accepted orders are automatically sent to the kitchen via KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket).

The system tracks order status from placement to preparation to delivery. Customers receive order confirmations and status updates. Restaurants can manage multiple orders simultaneously and prioritize orders based on preparation time and delivery schedules.

What channels does DineOpen support for online orders?

DineOpen online order management supports multiple channels including WhatsApp, QR menus, web platforms, and delivery apps. Customers can place orders through any of these channels, and all orders appear in the centralized POS system.

WhatsApp ordering allows customers to place orders directly through WhatsApp. QR menu ordering lets customers scan QR codes, view menus, and place orders. Web platform ordering enables customers to order through restaurant websites. Delivery app integration allows restaurants to receive orders from delivery platforms.
