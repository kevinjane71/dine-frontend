What is restaurant billing software?

Restaurant billing software is a system that generates bills, calculates taxes, processes payments, and maintains transaction records for restaurants. DineOpen billing software includes GST-ready features, automatic tax calculation, compliant invoice generation, and tax record maintenance for Indian restaurants.

How does restaurant billing software work?

Restaurant billing software processes customer orders, calculates totals including taxes, generates invoices, accepts payments, and maintains transaction records. DineOpen billing software automatically calculates GST at applicable rates, generates tax-compliant invoices with HSN codes, and maintains records required for tax filing.

When a customer places an order, the billing software records items, calculates subtotal, applies applicable taxes (GST), calculates final total, processes payment, and generates a receipt. All transactions are saved automatically and can be retrieved for reporting and tax filing.
